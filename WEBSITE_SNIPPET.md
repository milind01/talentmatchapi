### 🔍 Intelligent Search
Upload any document. Search like you're talking to an expert. Get instant, accurate answers from your entire document library.

## How It Works (4 Steps)

**1. Upload Documents**
Drop PDFs, Word docs, images - any format. DocAI instantly indexes and processes them.

**2. Ask Questions**
Natural language queries. "Find candidates with 5+ years Python experience" or "Analyze this contract for risks."

**3. Get Answers**
AI reasoning in real-time. Get comprehensive answers with source citations and confidence scores.

**4. Take Action**
Generate emails, export reports, integrate with your tools. Everything you need to act immediately.
